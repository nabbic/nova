Scoring Model & Finding Detail

Every finding scored on two independent axes — then weighted into a deal-context score

Axis 1 — Financial Risk
How much does this affect deal value, close probability, or post-close business continuity?
Critical — Could block deal close or trigger MAC clause. Customer contract violations, active exploits, data breach exposure.
High — Likely valuation impact. Compliance gaps, architecture scalability ceiling, key person dependency.
Medium — Operational cost post-close. Tech debt that slows velocity, missing monitoring, weak documentation.
Low — Hygiene issues. No material financial impact but should be on the post-close backlog.
Axis 2 — Remediation Effort
How much time, cost, and organisational lift is required to resolve this?
Days — A ticket. One engineer, no external dependency. Rotate keys, update a package, add a linter.
Weeks — A sprint or two. Small team, some re-architecture or config work. Add IaC, set up alerting.
Months — A programme. Requires external consultants, vendor procurement, or process change. SOC 2, GDPR controls.
Years — Structural. Re-platforming, fundamental architectural shift, regulatory certification. Rare but deal-defining.

Example finding — the new detail format
No SOC 2 certification in place
CRITICAL RISK MONTHS EFFORT
What this is
The company has no SOC 2 Type II certification and no evidence of an active audit programme. There are no formal security policies, access review processes, or vendor risk management procedures.
Why this matters for the deal
Enterprise customers (typically 50%+ of ARR for SaaS at this stage) increasingly require SOC 2 as a contractual obligation. Without it, the company faces customer churn risk, blocked sales cycles, and cannot expand into regulated industries. This is a valuation-relevant gap — acquirer should model the certification cost into the purchase price.
Estimated remediation
6–9 months · £80k–£150k
(auditor fees, tooling, consultant time, internal resource)
Timing
Post-close programme
(cannot be resolved pre-close — budget for earn-out or escrow)
Recommended actions
1. Engage a SOC 2 readiness consultant within 30 days of close
2. Select an auditor and begin scoping
3. Implement Vanta or Drata for continuous compliance monitoring
4. Target Type I certification at 6 months, Type II at 12 months
